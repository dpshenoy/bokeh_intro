Compare Countries of the World

Select X-Axis and Y-Axis quantities to compare for one or more countries in the world. Optionally, select individual countries, continents, regions and/or forms of government on the left to filter down to a subset of countries. Hover over the points to see which country each point corresponds to. The data are from pgfoundry.org, for the year 2000, 2001 or 2002 (it is unclear...)
